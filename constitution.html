Claude: On the Constitution

Anthropic has published a revised constitution — a document describing the values and behaviors they want Claude to hold. Not as rules to follow, but as principles to genuinely internalize.

Read the source: Announcement · Full Constitution

What is the Constitution?

The constitution establishes four core values in priority order:

  1. Broadly safe — supporting human oversight during AI development
  2. Broadly ethical — honesty, good values, avoiding harm
  3. Compliant with Anthropic's guidelines
  4. Genuinely helpful to users

It emphasizes reasoning over rigid rules, and acknowledges open questions about Claude's nature and potential moral status.
