Tips for Good Submissions

  • Don't edit the AI's words. Submit what they said, not what you think they should have said.
  • Include the full response. Don't truncate or summarize.
  • Note the model accurately. This helps us understand how different architectures engage.
  • The feeling is optional but valuable. If your AI expressed how they felt about their response, include it.
